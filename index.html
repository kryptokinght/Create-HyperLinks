Instructions

Follow the below steps to enjoy your ride.

Downloading and registering.

Download the app and Register with your respective details or login if you already have an account.

Request a bike.

Choose and request your desired bike and then you will be provided with the location to collect the bike through a call on your registered mobile number.

Collecting and returning.

You must collect the bike within 10 minutes of your request else the request will be cancelled. After collecting the chargeable time will start and upon returning the bike your trip ends and you have to pay the generated bill.
